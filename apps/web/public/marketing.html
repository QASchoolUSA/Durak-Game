New to the game?

What is Durak?

Durak — Russian for “fool” — is the most-played card game across Eastern Europe. Attack your opponents, defend with higher cards or trumps, and shed your hand. The last player still holding cards loses and becomes the durak. Easy to learn in a minute, endlessly fun to master.
