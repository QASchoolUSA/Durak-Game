Durak · Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

The short version: Durak doesn't require an account and isn't built to collect your personal information. We don't sell your data, we don't run third-party advertising trackers, and we only process the minimum needed to run online games. Read on for the details.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Durak card game app (“Durak,” “the app,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use it. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Display name. When you play online, you choose a display name so others in your room can identify you. This name does not need to be your real name and is not linked to a personal account.
  • Support messages. If you email us, we receive the contents of your message and your email address so we can reply.

Information created during gameplay

  • Game session data. To run online multiplayer, our backend temporarily processes game state — such as room codes, whose turn it is, and the cards in play — so all players stay in sync. This data is tied to a game session, not to your identity.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data. Like most apps, our service may process limited technical information (such as connection data and device type) needed to deliver and secure online play and to diagnose problems.
  • Diagnostics via Apple. If you opt in through your device settings, Apple may share aggregated crash and performance diagnostics with us. This is controlled by your iOS settings, not by us.

2. Push notifications

The app may ask permission to send push notifications (for example, to let you know it's your turn or that a friend joined your room). Notifications are optional — you can grant or deny permission when prompted, and change it any time in your device's Settings. If you deny permission, you can still play normally.

3. How we use information

  • To operate online multiplayer and keep all players' games in sync.
  • To provide, maintain, secure, and improve the app.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, abuse, or fraud.

We do not use your information for third-party advertising and we do not sell your personal information.

4. Service providers

We use trusted third-party infrastructure providers to deliver the app — for example, cloud hosting for real-time multiplayer and Apple's platform services for app distribution and notifications. These providers process data only on our behalf and only as needed to provide their services. They are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

5. Data retention

Game session data is transient and is retained only as long as needed to run active and recent games, after which it is deleted or de-identified in the ordinary course. Support emails are kept as long as needed to handle your request and our records.

6. Children's privacy

Durak is suitable for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

7. Your choices and rights

  • No account to delete. Because we don't tie gameplay to a personal account, there is generally no personal profile to access or erase.
  • Notifications. Manage or revoke notification permission in your device Settings at any time.
  • Requests. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To make a request, email us at the address below and we'll respond as required by applicable law.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International users

The app may be operated from, and information processed in, countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for any cross-border transfers of personal data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page, and your continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact us

Questions or requests about this policy or your data? Email us at support@durak.cards.
