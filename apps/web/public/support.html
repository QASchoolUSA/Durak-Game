Getting started

What is Durak?

Durak (Russian for “fool”) is one of the most popular card games in Eastern Europe. Players attack each other with cards and defend with higher cards of the same suit — or with trumps. The goal is simple: don't be the last player left holding cards. That last player is the durak.

How do I start a game?
  1. Open the app and tap PLAY.
  2. Choose Solo to play against AI opponents, or With friends to play online.
  3. Pick your settings (number of players, deck size, rules) and start the match.
Do I need an account?

No. You can play solo against AI instantly. For online play with friends, you just pick a display name — no email, password, or sign-up required.

Does it cost anything?

Durak is free to play. There are no forced ads interrupting your game and no pay-to-win mechanics.

Playing online with friends

How do I invite a friend?
  1. Tap PLAY → With friends → Create Room.
  2. Share the 6-digit room code with your friend (text, chat, anywhere).
  3. Your friend taps Join Game, enters the code and a display name.
  4. When everyone's in, tap Start Game. Empty seats are filled with AI.
Do we need to be on the same Wi-Fi?

No. Online matches run in the cloud, so you and your friends can play from anywhere in the world on Wi-Fi or cellular data.

My friend can't join with the room code
  • Double-check the code was typed correctly (it's case-insensitive but must be exact).
  • Room codes expire when the host leaves — have the host create a fresh room.
  • Make sure both players have an active internet connection.
  • Confirm you're both on the latest version of the app from the App Store.
What happens if someone disconnects?

The game waits briefly for players to reconnect. If a player can't return, their seat may be taken over by AI so the match can continue.

How to play (the rules)

The basics in 30 seconds
  • Each player gets 6 cards. One card is flipped to set the trump suit; the rest form the draw deck.
  • The attacker plays a card. The defender must beat it with a higher card of the same suit, or any trump.
  • Other players may throw in more cards of ranks already on the table.
  • If the defender beats everything, the cards are discarded. If they can't, they pick up all the cards.
  • Everyone refills to 6 cards from the deck. Play continues until the deck is empty and one player is left with cards — the durak.
What are the rule variants?
  • Podkidnoy (Throw-in): the classic version — attackers throw in matching ranks.
  • Perevodnoy (Pass / Transfer): you can pass the attack to the next player by adding a card of the same rank instead of defending.

You can choose the variant and deck size (24 / 36 cards) before starting a game.

How does the trump suit work?

Any trump card beats any card of the three normal suits. A higher trump beats a lower trump. The trump suit is shown on screen throughout the match.

Troubleshooting

The app is slow, frozen, or crashing
  1. Fully close the app (swipe it away in the app switcher) and reopen it.
  2. Make sure iOS and the app are both updated to the latest version.
  3. Restart your device if the problem persists.
  4. Still stuck? Email us with your device model and iOS version.
No sound or wrong volume
  • Check that your device isn't in silent mode and the volume is up.
  • Open the in-app Settings to toggle sound effects on or off.
Online games won't connect
  • Confirm you have a working internet connection (try loading a webpage).
  • Switch between Wi-Fi and cellular to rule out a network issue.
  • Disable any VPN or content blocker that might interfere with the connection.
How do I change themes or card designs?

Open Settings in the app. You can switch between multiple table appearances and card back designs to customize how the game looks.

Privacy & your data

What data does Durak collect?

Durak is built to be privacy-friendly. We don't require accounts and we don't collect personal information like your name, email, or contacts. Online play uses a display name you choose and temporary game state needed to run the match. See the full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I request data deletion?

Because we don't tie game data to a personal identity, there's typically nothing personal to delete. If you have a specific request, email support@durak.cards and we'll help.
